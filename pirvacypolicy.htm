About the App

Welcome to NumeroLuck! Generate personalised lottery numbers using your own data:

  • Birthday & life number (numerology)
  • Zodiac sign & favourite colour
  • Family birthdays & meaningful dates
  • Cultural background & personality type

Supports Lotto Max, Lotto 6/49, Ontario 49, Daily Grand and Lottario.

Choose Personal, Mixed, or Random mode.

All data stays private on your device — nothing is sent to any server.

Privacy Policy

Data collection: NumeroLuck does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All profile information (birthday, name, family members, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.
